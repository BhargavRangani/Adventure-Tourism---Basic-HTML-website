Camping in India If this holiday season, you wish to relax in the lap of the nature and want to enjoy in the wilderness, then camping in India sounds as the best idea. With its scenic environs and serene atmosphere, the country provides several hideouts from the chaotic city life. The thrilling and beautiful terrains of the Himalayas and Western Ghats present a perfect set-up for this activity. Not only mountainous regions, but the deserts and wildlife parks of India also offer an unforgettable experience. The breathtaking treks at places like Uttarakhand, Himachal, Maharashtra, West Bengal and Kerala are sure to attract you if you wish to explore the nature.
